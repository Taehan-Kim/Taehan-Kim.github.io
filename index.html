A Muffin to Die For

It was the perfect muffin. Baked with the rarest magical ingredients, the sorcerer had spent weeks perfecting the recipe. But before he could take a single bite...

CHOCO happened.

The mischievous little dog devoured the legendary pastry in one gulp. Driven mad by rage, the sorcerer imprisoned CHOCO in a mystical cage and unleashed hordes of terrifying monsters upon the peaceful town!

Now, it's up to you. Venture into the chaotic dungeons, defeat the monstrous legions, save CHOCO, and teach that bitter old sorcerer a lesson he'll never forget!
